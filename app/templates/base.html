A MINUMENT (or a mini-monument) is a creative piece, a small 3D-printed recreation of a monument (in the real-world or not) resting on a 2" by 2" base. You can find a catalogue of these on this website's storefront - you can also favourite items and purchase them if you're logged in (with all of them each costing the same - fair pricing!). You might need to register an account under REGISTER, at the top of the page, if you don't have one registered already.
